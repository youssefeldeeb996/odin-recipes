Description

This is an iced coffee recipe

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee
  • Hot water
  • Cold water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Add the instant coffee to a cup
  2. Add two tbs of sugar
  3. Add the hot water just enough to dissolve te coffee
  4. Add half a cup of cold water
  5. Add 1/3 a cup of milk
  6. Add enough ice cubes
  7. ENJOY!
